To import an existing Stellar Lumens wallet, please enter your 12 word seed phrase.
Enter the entire phrase, all lowercase, and with a space between each word.

Alternatively, you may enter the 56 character secret key associated with your wallet.

Secret keys begin with an S and consist of 56 alphanumeric characters.

Please note that wallets created with other services/apps may not be importable.
Other wallets may use some other seed phrase length, such as 15 or 24 words.
In this case, your secret key is how you will access those funds.
