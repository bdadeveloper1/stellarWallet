Q&A

The Crypto$hare wallet is just a wallet. Your keys are securely stored in your browser's cookies. As such, Crypto$hare does NOT have access to your keys. We want to give users the most control over their funds. You can purchase XLM directly from us, but we do not manage these funds. They will go directly to your wallet.
Stellar's blockchain provides fast and extremely cheap transactions.
Ether gas fees can get quite high during times of congestion, and we wouldn't want users to lose money to gas fees.
Additionally, choosing to use less gas causes your transaction to take longer to be approved. Likewise, BSC transaction fees can spike during high transactions periods.

Stellar's transaction fee has nearly always been so cheap that it would take 10,000 transactions to spend, in total, 1 XLM in fees.
